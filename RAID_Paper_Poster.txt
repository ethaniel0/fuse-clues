Pratt School of Engineering — ECE MS Poster Session

Low-Power RAID Architectures for Embedded Systems  
M. Palmeri, A. Cook

------------------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW
We present a low-power RAID architecture optimized for
embedded deployments using distributed parity and routing-
aware recovery.

------------------------------------------------------------

KEY RESULTS
• 40% reduction in write latency  
• 18% faster recovery  
• Stable performance across 236 test nodes  

------------------------------------------------------------

SYSTEM DESIGN
• Rotating parity blocks across nodes  
• No dedicated parity disk  
• Routing-tag-based recovery abstraction  

Each deployment is associated with a routing tag used during
checkpoint-based recovery.
